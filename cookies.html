Cookies Policy

At Garden Glow (gardenglow.site), we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and provide personalized content. This policy explains how we use cookies and your choices regarding them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, improve performance, and provide a better user experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use
  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly, such as maintaining login sessions or remembering form inputs.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze website traffic and improve site performance.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences, such as language settings or location.
  • Marketing Cookies: Track your browsing habits to show relevant content and advertisements.
3. Managing Cookies

You can choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to manage cookies, delete existing cookies, or set notifications when a cookie is set. Please note that disabling some cookies may affect your experience on our website.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services such as analytics or advertising partners, which may also use cookies to collect information about your interactions with our site. We do not control these third-party cookies, and their use is subject to their own privacy policies.
